Sharpens what's there.

Fidelity-first losses, a low adversarial weight, and a physics downsample-consistency constraint. Every result ships with a residual audit and Kelvin-unit metrics, so a reviewer can verify the model added no structure the input didn't support.

03 — THE MODELS

Swappable, config-driven.

SUPER-RESOLUTION

EDSR & SwinIR

A fast residual-CNN baseline, or a compact window-attention transformer. Charbonnier pixel loss + optional VGG perceptual + physics consistency — adversarial weight kept low to prioritise radiometric fidelity over invented texture.

Charbonnier VGG perceptual downsample-consistency
COLORIZATION

pix2pix cGAN

A U-Net generator with a PatchGAN discriminator. L1 content + adversarial realism + optional perceptual / SSIM. Trains on the real 100 m HR TIR for a clean ablation, or on the upsampled LR for the composed pipeline.

U-Net PatchGAN L1 + adversarial
Inference is tiled with overlap and feathered cosine blending to remove seams — streamed tile-by-tile to bound memory, with AMP/half precision on CUDA. infer/ pipeline.run()
